# 📖 IELTS Learning Platform - User Manual

This manual provides instructions for Students, Teachers, and Administrators on how to use the IELTS Learning Platform.

---

## 👨‍🎓 1. For Students (Mobile & Desktop)

### Getting Started

- **Account Creation**: Register using your email address. Verify your account via the OTP (One-Time Password) sent to your inbox.
- **Google Sign-In**: Alternatively, use your Google account for a one-tap login experience.

### Studying Modules

- **Reading & Listening**: Access categorized practice tests. Select a difficulty level that matches your current proficiency.
- **Writing Practice**: 
  - Choose a Topic (Task 1 or Task 2).
  - Type your essay directly in the interface.
  - Choose **AI Grading** for instant feedback or **Human Grading** to have a teacher review it.
- **Vocabulary Builder**: Learn new words with AI-generated examples and practice quizzes.

### i-Coins & Pro Membership

- Earn i-Coins by completing tests or purchase them via the shop.
- Upgrade to **PRO** to unlock full mock exams and unlimited AI writing evaluations.

---

## 👩‍🏫 2. For Teachers (Teacher Portal)

### Dashboard Overview

- View pending writing submissions from students.
- Monitor student progress and common mistakes.

### Grading Workspace

- **Claiming Submissions**: Select a student essay from the queue to start grading.
- **Feedback Tools**: Use the rich text editor to highlight errors and provide detailed improvement tips.
- **Scoring**: Assign band scores based on official IELTS criteria.

### Content Creation

- Add new vocabulary items or practice questions to the platform.
- Review AI-generated content to ensure educational accuracy.

---

## ⚙️ 3. For Administrators (Admin Dashboard)

### User Management

- View and manage all platform users (Students, Teachers, and other Admins).
- Update user roles and track account status.

### Platform Monitoring

- **Financial Reports**: Monitor i-Coin transactions and subscription revenue.
- **System Logs**: View real-time activity and security logs.
- **Legal & Policies**: Update the Terms of Service and Privacy Policy directly from the dashboard.

### Moderation

- Review flagged content or reported user interactions.
- Manage global announcements and system-wide notifications.
